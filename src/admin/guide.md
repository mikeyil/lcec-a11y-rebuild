  ## Welcome to the LC Education Consulting Content Editor

  This is where you can update text, images, and settings on the website without touching any code. Changes you save here are sent to the live site automatically — the site rebuilds in about 2–3 minutes after each save.

  ---

  ## How to make a change

  1. Click the section you want to edit in the left sidebar (Settings, Pages, or Testimonials).
  2. Find the field you want to change and update it.
  3. Click **Save** in the upper right.
  4. Wait 2–3 minutes, then refresh the live site to see your change.

  That's it. You do not need to publish or deploy anything — saving is all it takes.

  ---

  ## What each section does

  **Settings**
  - **Site Info** — The booking URL (used by all CTA buttons), phone, email, and city. Update the Booking URL here if your scheduling link ever changes.
  - **Office / Contact Info** — The phone number, email, and location shown on the Contact page.
  - **Announcement Bar** — The slim banner at the top of every page. Leave the text blank to hide it.
  - **Global CTA Block** — The green "Ready to Get Started?" section at the bottom of most pages.
  - **Navigation** — The links in the top navigation bar.

  **Testimonials** — Add, edit, or reorder client quotes. The Full Quote appears on the Why Choose Us page; the Short Quote is used on the Homepage.

  **Pages** — Edit the content of each individual page: Homepage, Our Story, Why Choose Us, Accessibility Services, Webinars & Training, Portfolio, Contact, and the Contact Success page.

  ---

  ## Tips

  - **Some paragraphs contain links written as HTML** (they look like `<a href="/page/">text</a>`). If you see this in a paragraph field, keep the tag exactly as written — just update the surrounding words if needed. Do not delete the tag.
  - **Images** on the Our Story and Portfolio pages can be replaced by clicking the image field and uploading a new file.
  - **The booking URL** (the link for all "Schedule Call" buttons) is set once in Settings → Site Info → Booking URL. You do not need to update it on each page separately.
  - If something looks wrong after saving, wait a full 3 minutes and do a hard refresh (Ctrl+Shift+R on Windows, Cmd+Shift+R on Mac).
